## Starting Point / 前期想法

Motor-driver experiments can look successful too early. Once the motor spins, the experiment feels alive, but wiring mistakes, unstable power, unsuitable PWM frequency, and noisy feedback can still hide underneath.

这篇先作为实验记录模板，不写成完整教程。每次调电机，都应该留下驱动板型号、供电、电机参数、PWM 频率、反馈方式和异常现象。不然过几天再看，只剩一句“好像能转”。

## Question / 疑问：电机能转不就行了吗？

Not enough. Rotation is only the first signal. Engineering work needs to know how it starts, whether low speed is stable, whether feedback is believable, and whether load changes break the loop.

能转只是第一步。更重要的是怎么转：启动有没有冲击，低速稳不稳，反馈准不准，负载一变会不会抽风。后续如果要做闭环控制，前面这些假设不写清楚，调参会变成玄学。

## Stage Goals / 阶段目标

- Record wiring and power configuration.
- Record PWM frequency, duty cycle, and motor response.
- Identify speed-feedback source: Hall sensor, encoder, or estimation.
- Add oscilloscope captures, parameter tables, and abnormal-case notes.

## Reflection / 复盘

Motor control is not just making something spin. The harder part is making it controllable, measurable, and repeatable. FOC and fan-driver content can later connect to this learning line.
