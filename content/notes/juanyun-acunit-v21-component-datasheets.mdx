## Why This Note Exists / 为什么写这篇

The V2.1 board split is only meaningful if the component constraints are traceable. I grouped the key datasheets here so the project page can explain why the board needs separate power handling, protected signal paths, Wi-Fi reserve, EEV driving, fan control, and sensor inputs.

V2.1 拆成主控板、分电板、交互板，不只是为了排版更整齐，而是因为器件约束会把系统自然拆开。我把关键规格书整理在这里，方便后续把板级设计和固件联调讲清楚。

## What I Mapped / 我关注的内容

- PWM fan and pressure transmitter parameters define signal ranges and control/measurement expectations.
- ESP-01S affects UART allocation, 3.3 V supply stability, antenna clearance, and firmware communication reserve.
- TPS54560, LGS5145, and AMS1117 affect the power tree and local regulation strategy.
- ULN2003A explains the EEV/stepper drive path and why the firmware needs a stepped control abstraction.
- DHT11, 2N7002, SMAJ12A, and SMBJ30A connect the low-speed sensor path, level/signal conditioning, and surge/ESD protection discussion.

## Reflection / 复盘

This note makes the case study less like a picture gallery and more like an engineering archive. The next step is to connect each datasheet group to a concrete design decision in the schematic: connector choice, protection device placement, power rail margin, and firmware initialization order.

这篇让项目不只是图片展示，而更像工程归档。后续我应该把每组器件资料继续映射到原理图决策：接口怎么选、保护器件放哪里、电源裕量怎么估、固件初始化为什么按那个顺序来。
