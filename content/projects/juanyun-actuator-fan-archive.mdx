## Project Brief / 项目简介

This archive groups thinner actuator-control experiments: solenoid-valve control and a quiet single-phase BLDC fan-driver board line. It is useful as a future direction, but the public portfolio should not publish raw company files.

这条线后续可以补接线记录、时序图、电流测量、实物照片和脱敏代码片段。当前不公开 `.ioc`、`.c`、原理图、BOM 或 EDA 工程。
