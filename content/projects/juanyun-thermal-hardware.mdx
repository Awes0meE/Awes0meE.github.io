## Project Brief / 项目简介

This page is intentionally a public-safe overview. The raw Juanyun folder contains real company engineering artifacts: board exports, firmware source, datasheets, SOP documents, prototype records, and manufacturing files. Those files are useful evidence for my own archive, but many of them are not suitable for a public portfolio.

这页现在不再直接公开 Gerber、原理图、BOM、贴片坐标、源码和完整 PDF。它先保留项目方向、我做过的工作范围和后续可以公开补证据的位置。真正需要给导师、HR 或面试官看的版本，可以之后单独准备脱敏材料。

## What I Worked On / 我做过的方向

- STM32-based control-board firmware organization.
- AC-unit control-board requirement reading, interface split, and board-version documentation.
- Base-unit sensing, display, fan-control, Bluetooth reporting, and parameter persistence.
- Hardware-development SOP writing and project handoff documentation.
- Smaller sensor, actuator, cooling-prototype, and motor-control archive lines.

## Public Evidence Boundary / 公开证据边界

For now I only keep a very small public evidence set. Company-specific production files stay out of the deployed website:

- no Gerber archives;
- no schematic PDFs;
- no BOM or pick-and-place files;
- no EasyEDA/CAD source projects;
- no complete firmware source dumps;
- no internal requirement or manufacturing packages.

## Next Step / 后续补充

后续如果要公开展示这段经历，优先补脱敏后的照片、系统框图重绘版、个人复盘、测试记录截图和不含公司机密的说明图。这样能证明我参与过真实工程，而不是把公司的完整设计文件直接扔到网上。
