## Project Brief / 项目简述

This page is the umbrella for the Juanyun internship archive. It intentionally stays at overview level. The source material covers current product boards and firmware, legacy prototypes, small sensor / actuator tasks, FOC experiments, datasheets, and hardware-development process documents. Some of that material is safe to discuss publicly; some of it is product-sensitive and should stay outside the website.

公开页的目标不是把公司文件夹搬上网，而是讲清楚做过哪些方向、哪些证据可以公开、哪些材料必须收住。当前产品相关的 ACUnit / BaseUnit 内容只保留脱敏截图和叙述，不提供 Gerber、原理图、BOM/PnP、EDA/CAD、完整固件源码或制造资料下载。其他卷云 legacy 项目按用户重新划定的边界可以公开，当前页面只链接筛选后的关键证据，不发布未整理的整包目录。

## Public Scope / 公开范围

- Thermal control-board overview: board version screenshots, high-level block diagrams, and public notes.
- Base-unit firmware overview: sensing, display, fan control, Bluetooth reporting, and parameter persistence as public narrative only.
- Legacy prototype lines: DIY pressure-flow cooling prototype, FOC driver-board archive, actuator / fan archive, and DHT sensor breakout plan.
- Process material: the self-authored hardware development SOP, published as a public note and PDF.

## Public Evidence Boundary / 公开证据边界

Served publicly:

- selected screenshots / board renders already under `/uploads/projects/juanyun-tech/`;
- the DIY demo video, 3D-print preview screenshots, and selected DIY source / PCB / fabrication files;
- selected FOC driver Gerber, BOM/PnP, InteractiveBOM, EasyEDA, STEP, datasheets, and source excerpts;
- selected solenoid-valve source / IOC files, OLED / EC11 schematics, ULN2003 reference, and BLDC fan BOM / EasyEDA files;
- the DHT task-plan PDF and cover preview;
- the self-authored hardware SOP PDF and cover image.

Not served publicly:

- Current_Product_ACUnit_Project raw Gerber, schematic, BOM/PnP, EDA/CAD, firmware, and manufacturing packages;
- Current_Product_BaseUnit_Project STM32 firmware source tree, vendor packages, generated build output, and internal handoff files;
- legacy installers, dependency folders, vendor folders, generated build output, duplicate exports, and uncurated complete source trees.

## Reflection / 复盘

这页更像目录，不是单个项目的战报。卷云材料的难点是边界：公开太少会像空壳，公开太多又会把公司工程文件暴露出去。现在的做法是把可公开的学习线和流程线写实，把当前产品线保持在总览和脱敏截图层级。
