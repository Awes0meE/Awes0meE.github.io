## Project Brief / 项目简介

This case study records the engineering layer behind the SAT301 graduation thesis: sensing, mapping, planning, and motion control inside a structured robot-navigation workflow.

这一部分后续会补充论文之外的工程视角：系统结构、关键模块、实验环境、失败记录、调试过程和最终效果。

## Scope / 工作范围

- Perception, mapping, navigation, and control modules connected into one experiment flow.
- Planning and control behavior observed under limited sensor and compute resources.
- Thesis material reorganized into reusable robotics and SLAM notes.
