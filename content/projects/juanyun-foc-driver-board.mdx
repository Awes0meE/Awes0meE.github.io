## Project Brief / 项目简介

This line may later become a useful bridge between embedded hardware and motor-control learning. For now, the public page does not expose raw manufacturing files or source files.

这条线后续适合认真补成 FOC 学习/项目复盘，但公开之前需要补测试波形、采样链路解释、保护逻辑和脱敏后的图。直接把 Gerber、BOM、EasyEDA 工程和源码放公网不合适。
